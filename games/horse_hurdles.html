3D Horse Hurdle Run

Guide a 3D horse down the track, dodge sideways, and jump hurdles at speed.

This is a self-contained 3D browser game. The horse is built from shaded 3D geometry with lighting and animation, so it feels much more like a real 3D scene.

A D ← → Space

Move left and right to line up, then press Space to jump the hurdles.

Save as horse_3d_game.html and open it in Chrome or Safari on your Mac.
